Studio Mod.o is an architecture studio based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The three architects have different experiences and points of view, so together they are able to create sophisticated, functional and contemporary projects. We created their name merging two concepts: 'modificar' (to modify, in portuguese), one of the main skills of an architect, since they can modify an environment to better suit its use, while providing a good experience; and 'modo' (mode, in portuguese), representing their own point of view. We also created their logo and visual identity.
