Studio NK Design

A new identity to communicate their true personality and style.

branding.
graphic design.

Context.

Studio NK Design is an architecture studio that offers interior design projects online. They needed a new visual identity that would communicate their personality and design style that is clean and geometric.
@studionkdesign

Process.

We worked together with the architects on a mood board, so we could combine our style with their personality.

The symbol unifies the letters N and K with a three-dimensional representation of a room.

Role.

This project was a collaboration with @biamilhomem.

Together we work as the design collective Northwest.

We shared the same responsibilities in every part of this project: logo design, visual identity, collateral materials, and presentations to the client.
